Understanding Your weight across the Cosmos

in this interactive weight calculator you are able to see your weight on different planets, but why do we have a different weight on a different planet?
This happens because each planet in the solar systems possesses a unique gravitational pull, this is primarily determined by it's mass and radius. A good example of this is Jupiter, the largest planet in our solar system, it exerts a much stronger gravitational forcer than earth, making you heavier if you manage to get to it's surface.
This project aims to offer a tangible sense of how your mass, can weight differently accross the solar system, thanks to the varying gravitational forces, it's a simple yet profound way to demostrate gravity, and exploring this elemental forces help us understand a bit better the forces that form our solar system and our universe!
